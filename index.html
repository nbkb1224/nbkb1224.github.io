About LeBron James

LeBron Raymone James Sr. is an American professional basketball player who is a free agent. He has played in the National Basketball Association for the Cleveland Cavaliers and Miami Heat James first garnered national attention as the top high school basketball player in the country. With his unique combination of size, athleticism and court vision, he became a four-time NBA MVP. After leading the Miami Heat to titles in 2012 and 2013, James returned to Cleveland and helped the franchise claim its first championship in 2016.In January 2018, at age 33, LeBron James surpassed Kobe Bryant as the youngest player to accumulate 30,000 career points and became the seventh player in NBA history to achieve that milestone. The feat put him just more than 8,000 points shy of Kareem Abdul-Jabbar's all-time record of 38,387 points..
